Why we exist
ORIGIN.

Many Defence programmes invest heavily in digital transformation — and then struggle to translate policy, security frameworks and architecture into working platforms that are accredited, stable and fit for mission use.

Northforge was founded by practitioners who have spent decades inside Australian Defence and national security programmes — designing classified environments, running secure cloud transitions, and producing the accreditation evidence that gets systems into operation.

That experience taught us something important: the gap between secure digital strategy and operational capability is bridged by people, not by frameworks. By practitioners who understand the security policy, the Microsoft platform, and the operational realities of complex programmes — and who can carry context across all three.

Northforge exists to close that gap. We design, build and operate sovereign digital platforms aligned to Australian Government security frameworks. We do not write strategy documents that someone else has to implement. We do the implementation.

What we believe
FOUR CONVICTIONS
01
SECURITY IS
FOUNDATIONAL

Security is the foundation a platform is built on — not a layer applied at the end. Every architectural decision Northforge makes is grounded in the relevant security framework before it’s grounded in technology preference. The ISM is not a final-stage compliance exercise. It is a design input.

02
ACCREDITATION
IS A METHOD

Authority to operate is not a documentation exercise that follows the build. It is a method that runs alongside the build. The Forge Method™ produces SSPS, SoA, SRA and control evidence as architecture and implementation happen — not afterward. The result is faster, cleaner, more defensible accreditations.

03
SOVEREIGNTY
MATTERS

Sovereign capability is not a marketing claim. It is a structural property: who owns the company, who delivers the work, where the work happens, who holds the clearances, who carries the legal accountability. Northforge is 100% Australian owned, Australian operated, and Australian delivered. By design, not by accident.

04
PRACTITIONERS
OVER ADVISORS

There is no shortage of advisors in the Australian Defence digital market. There is a shortage of practitioners who can take a brief and ship the platform. Northforge is built around practitioner depth. The team that scopes the engagement is the team that delivers it — not a separate downstream resource pool.

Where we work
CANBERRA. AUSTRALIA.

Northforge is based in Canberra. We serve Defence, Federal Government, National Security and Defence Industry programmes across Australia — with proximity to the policy environment, the customer base and the cleared talent pool.

Australian-owned, ABN-registered, Australian-domiciled tax residency. The work is performed in Australia, by Australians, under Australian law.

For programmes with sensitivities around FOCI, supply chain or jurisdictional risk, this matters — and we structure every engagement to make it auditable.
